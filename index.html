AETTS

Audio extraction, transcription, translation and synthesis in one lean toolkit.

1. Extract Audio

Grab audio from video files with a single command.

2. Remove Silence

Automatically remove long pauses and split the audio into chunks.

3. Transcribe

Run Whisper locally to transcribe the audio.

4. Translate

Convert German transcripts into English.

5. Synthesize Speech

Generate lifelike speech with Kokoro TTS.

Features

  • Extract audio from video files.
  • Silence removal and chunking.
  • Offline Whisper transcription.
  • German to English translation.
  • Speech synthesis with Kokoro TTS.
